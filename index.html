VR Traveling Bitstarter helps our geek team to raise funds for the fasinating and stunning project of VR Traveling. The VR Traveling is used by common people who cannot afford high cost of traveling around the world or who would like to pre-view the potential targeted traveling places before planning the vacation. This software, with Oculus VR, Samsung Gear VR or Sony Morpheus VR, can give people a stunning 360-degree view of target places in the world. The goal of this project is to provide fantastic experience for people to experience the world full of fun.

The VR industry is growing at a fast pace, with revenues from virtual reality products (both hardware and software) projected to increase from 90 million U.S. dollars in 2014 to 5.2 billion U.S. dollars in 2018. The number of active VR users is forecast to reach 171 million by 2018. According to recent forecasts, revenue from virtual reality head-mounted displays is expected to grow from 685 million U.S. dollars in 2015 to 3.89 billion U.S. dollars in 2018.
